What is Luno Login?

Luno is a global cryptocurrency platform that allows users to buy, sell, and store various digital currencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. Logging into Luno is a straightforward process, but it involves several steps designed to ensure the security of your account.

Steps to Log In to Luno

  1. Visit the Luno Website or App:
    • To log in, you first need to access Luno either via their official website or the mobile app. The website can be found at Luno.com, and the app is available for download on both iOS and Android devices.
  2. Enter Your Credentials:
    • Once on the login page, you'll be prompted to enter your email address and password. These credentials should have been set up when you first registered for your Luno account.
  3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
    • For added security, Luno may prompt you to complete a Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) step. This involves entering a code sent to your registered mobile number or email address, or generated by an authenticator app. This step is crucial in protecting your account from unauthorized access.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

If you encounter any issues during the login process, there are several steps you can take:

  • Forgotten Password: You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
  • Account Lockout: After multiple failed login attempts, your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons. If this happens, follow the instructions provided by Luno to regain access.
  • Contact Support: If all else fails, you can reach out to Luno's customer support for assistance.

Security Tips

  • Always use a strong, unique password.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and only log in through the official Luno website or app.
